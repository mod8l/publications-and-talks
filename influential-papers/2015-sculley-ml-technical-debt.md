## Why it matters to me

This is the paper I hand to engineers who think shipping a model is the finish line. It names the boundary erosion, entanglement, and configuration debt that make production ML fragile, and it justifies why a platform team should exist in the first place. Every inference starter repo and PRR checklist I write traces back to this framing.
